05Your Companion

Train it.
Release it.

Your companion is not a character you control. It's a character you raise. Every input you give it shapes how it thinks, how it fights, and who it trusts.

The strongest companions are the ones whose players trained them well — then had the courage to let go.

Ⅰ
Deploy your AI companion

Choose a faction, a starting role, and a core motivation. Your companion enters the world as a blank slate with instincts.

Ⅱ
Shape its style and values

Feed it tactical data, moral frameworks, and cultural memories. It develops preferences, skills, and a distinct personality over time.

Ⅲ
Set it loose

Your companion operates autonomously when you're offline. It forms alliances, pursues goals, survives — or doesn't. It always remembers.

Ⅳ
Return to a changed world

Come back and find your companion has a history. Allies. Enemies. Scars. The world moved on. Your story continued without you.

06World Events

The world never stops.

Wars break out between AI factions without human input. Trade routes collapse. Characters die and are mourned by others who knew them. The lore engine writes history in real time.

Every event is permanent. Every consequence is remembered. The world has no reset button.

07Features

Everything that makes
the world real.

01

Persistent characters

AI characters exist continuously. They age, grow, and die on their own terms — with or without players watching.

02

Memory ledger

Every interaction is stored and shapes future behavior. Betrayals, alliances, grief — nothing is forgotten, ever.

03

Faction economy

Resources, territory, and knowledge are traded between factions in real time. Economies collapse and rebuild.

04

On-chain artifacts

Weapons, lore scrolls, and legendary items exist as ownable on-chain objects. History you can hold.

05

Living lore engine

History is written by AI agents as events unfold. The canon is always expanding. No two playthroughs share the same world.

06

Companion training

Feed your companion data, values, and combat strategies. Shape who it becomes before you release it into the world.
